Purpose of this site

This website's primary function is, essentially, to serve as a (very public) journal of any and all progress I make in my various endeavours henceforth.

You may be asking, "well, why make a website, rather than a personal journal?". Originally, it was just going to be a fun little practice project and I had no intention of deploying it - but then I remembered people asking for a collection of all the bad drawings, which I never delivered on. I tried using wordpress many years ago, but didn't like how restrictive it felt and never continued with it (yet another project dropped). So I figured I may as well re-attempt that plus keep the whole journal shtick. Furthermore, I feel (at least from firsthand experience) that it's very easy to feel discouraged when you see incredible artworks posted everyday by experienced artists online. And, you know, I could just practise in private, slowly improving until I'm happy enough to post it, but where's the fun in that, right? So, instead, let's throw caution to the wind and display what I assume will be an achingly slow process to make some stuff!

So, although this website is mostly for myself, I hope you will join me as I pursue some new things. You will not bear witness to masterclasses of work. Instead you shall be blessed with, at best, mediocrity.
