The website has launched, hooray! Today's update/random thought is, believe it or not, about this website! Fun, right?

The primary function of this website was not intended to be a drawings compilation - in fact, it was never intended to go public in the first place - but halfway through development, I thought it a good idea to add them. After all, like it or not, the 'Drawing Badly' thing WAS over two years of my life. It seems wasteful to not at least feature it. Then I realised there wouldn't be a whole lot of point in keeping this private if I want it to house all my drawings, so... we've ended up here.

Actually trying to include it and make it work in a way that didn't involve copy-paste static html was certainly an interesting experience and something I didn't expect to be quite as much of a pain as it turned out to be. But I gleaned much off of this process, and have learnt a fair bit on web development now. Doesn't that interest you, Mr./Ms./Mx. potential employer reading this?

I won't beat around the bush. This website is not a paragon of web design, but for a first (proper) attempt, I'm quite pleased with how it turned out. HTML, JavaScript, CSS - all done completely from the ground up... and a lot of tutorials. Enjoy the site and its somewhat limited offerings!

I ought to add that for a large majority of the drawings, I no longer have the original versions (I was running out of storage space). This means they're not as high quality as I'd like, hence I've provided links to their respective Reddit posts in the image notes, which should look a little nicer than on here.

(Making notes for over 900 images were such a long process, oh my GOD, why do I do this to myself?)

Anyway, we're here now, and I've delivered a drawing compilation three years too late. Better late than never though, I suppose.
